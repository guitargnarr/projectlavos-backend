## The Psychology of Question-as-Answer

### Why Answering a Question with a Question Works

**1. Pattern Interrupt:**
- Expected: "Yes, here's how AI helps..."
- Actual: "Well, what problem are you solving?"
- Result: Attention captured, defense lowered

**2. Socratic Method:**
- You're not telling them what they need
- You're helping them discover it themselves
- Discovery creates ownership (not sales resistance)

**3. Qualification Filter:**
- Vague answer = not ready (don't waste time)
- Specific answer = ready to buy (high intent)
- Saves both parties time

**4. Expertise Signal:**
- Vendors give answers
- Consultants ask questions
- Immediately positions you as expert advisor

**5. Memorable:**
- "That AI consultant who answered with a question"
- Easier to refer ("He'll ask you what takes 10 hours")
- Sticks in mind (most pitches are forgettable)
